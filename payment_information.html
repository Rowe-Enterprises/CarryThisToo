FAQ

Q: Why was I charged tax for my purchase?

A: Tax payments are not decided by Carry This Too. Your state or city requires tax payment for subscription services.

Q: I canceled my service. Why did I get charge?

A: You canceled your service after your current paid period ended and you began a new paid period. You should not be billed for the period that follows.

Q: Why was my card charged? I canceled my free-trail premium subscription.

A: You canceled your service after the free-trail period ended. You began a paid subscription when your free-trail offer ended.

Q: Why have I been charged by Carry This Too?

A: We offer a free service and paid service. A charge from Carry This Too means that you purchased one of our paid products.

Note: If you signed up for a trial, a paid subscription begins when the free-trail offer ends.

Q: Why am I paying more than $1.99 per month?

A: Our fees may be subject to regional taxes, inflation, or tax increases. If you’ve moved to a new place you may be subjected to additional fees. Check subscription tax requirements with your local and or state authorities. At times, we may be required to increase our fees. If we increase our fees you will be notified in advance.

Q: Why do I have this small charge on my bank statement?

A: When you submit your payment information to Carry This Too™, your banking institution may apply a small temporary hold to verify your card. This small charge should be refunded according to your banking institutions policy.
