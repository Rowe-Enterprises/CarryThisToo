Why Carry This Too™?

Just like you, the creator of this application was not only concerned with arming-up for safety, but also was wary of the concealed carry laws that change so rapidly. Firearm criminal charges come with hefty penalties. Ignorance is never a defense against the law. Our mission is to keep you safe and in the know.

We work closely with licensed firearm instructors, in every state, all around the country, to make sure you are getting the most up-to-date information. You should maintain a close relationship with your instructor. I have my instructor’s phone number in my phone. You should too. Gun safety training is an on-going process. This app does not replace one-on-one training. We merely provide information. Remember, guns don’t kill people. People kill people. Be Safe!
